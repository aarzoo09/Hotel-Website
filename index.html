The History Of Our restaurant

Nestled in the heart of Sultanpur, The Enchanted Table stands as a testament to history, elegance, and unparalleled hospitality. Over the decades,The Enchanted Table underwent several transformations and expansions, each phase adding a layer of grandeur and sophistication to its architecture and services. The roaring twenties witnessed a significant refurbishment, where the hotel's ballrooms and suites were redesigned to exude opulence and cater to the elite of society.
